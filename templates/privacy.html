Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking features of Spendly. This includes displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use essential infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) to operate the app. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
